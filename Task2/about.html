About Me

LC Photography is a small business started and fuelled by a passion for horses and photography. Lauren is a 20 year old from Johannesburg who took a liking to photography at the age of 13, and her passion has grown ever since into what it is today. She has been an avid horse rider for over 12 years, which gave her a starting point for photographic experimenting and sparked her interest for her primary photography type, equine photography. Her experience ranges from horse portraits, to family portraits, to wildlife and everything in between. Get in contact with Lauren today to book your photoshoot!
I am based in Johannesburg, Gauteng. I travels all over Gauteng for private shoots and show coverings. My hobby has grown into a large business that is continuing to grow.
